Why this website exists

6 July 2026

This website is a compact public research profile for my academic CV, research interests, selected work, and developing direction in computational biology and in silico drug discovery.

The goal is to present my chemistry background together with the computational themes I am building toward: cancer genomics, Beacon API, Progenetix, molecular docking, molecular dynamics simulations, and virtual screening.

It is intentionally minimal, public-only, and focused on research communication rather than visitor accounts or forms.
