Research Focus

Research Roadmap

A focused direction for developing work at the intersection of computational biology, cancer genomics, and structure-based in silico drug discovery.

Core themes

The roadmap keeps the site connected to ongoing learning, research assistance, and future project development.

Cancer Genomics

Study large-scale genomic datasets, especially copy number variations and their relationship to cancer progression.

Biomedical Data Access

Work with standards such as Beacon API and resources such as Progenetix for interoperable biomedical data sharing.

In Silico Discovery

Develop molecular docking, molecular dynamics, and virtual screening workflows for bioactive compounds.

Next steps

  1. Strengthen computational foundations

    Continue building practical skills in Python, statistics, biological data analysis, and reproducible research workflows.

  2. Develop Beacon API and Progenetix work

    Explore standardized endpoint access, cancer genomic datasets, and copy number variation analysis for research and teaching materials.

  3. Connect molecular data with drug design

    Use chemistry and biochemistry knowledge to frame molecular docking, MD simulations, and virtual screening studies.

  4. Write public research notes

    Turn reading, course work, and project progress into short notes that document the research path.
